What is Chatyour?

Chatyour is a real-time chat application designed from the ground up with accessibility as its core principle. It provides a seamless chat experience for blind and visually impaired users through deep screen reader integration, keyboard-driven navigation, and audio feedback.

Unlike mainstream chat platforms that often treat accessibility as an afterthought, Chatyour is built around it — every feature, every interaction is designed to work effortlessly with screen readers and text-to-speech software.

Why Chatyour?

Built for blind users, by people who understand accessibility. Chatyour integrates with accessible_output2 for instant speech feedback, so you never need to look at the screen to know what's happening.
  • Full screen reader support — All UI elements are properly labeled and announced.
  • Text-to-speech feedback — Messages, status updates, and alerts are spoken aloud automatically.
  • Keyboard-first navigation — Every action has a keyboard shortcut. No mouse required.
  • Automatic reconnection — If the connection drops, Chatyour reconnects in the background without losing your place.
  • Persistent chat rooms — Rooms can be saved and restored, so conversations are never lost.
  • Admin tools — Full moderation controls including bans, role management, and server announcements.

How It Helps

For blind and visually impaired individuals, most chat platforms present significant barriers — inaccessible interfaces, poorly labeled controls, and features that require visual interaction. Chatyour eliminates these barriers entirely:

  • Log in, navigate rooms, send messages, and manage your account all without ever looking at the screen.
  • Real-time speech alerts keep you informed of new messages, room changes, and server events.
  • The interface is streamlined and consistent, reducing cognitive load and learning curve.
  • Automatic reconnection means you stay in the conversation even with an unstable internet connection.
